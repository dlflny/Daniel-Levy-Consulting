BACKGROUND

About Daniel

Daniel C. Levy is an experienced business leader with a background in finance, telecommunications, and entrepreneurship. He graduated with honors from Baruch College, earning a degree in Finance and Investments.

Daniel began his career as an intern at a national telecommunications company and rose to become Vice President of Sales. In that role, he helped build the company's sales organization and played a key part in generating tens of millions of dollars in revenue. He continues to serve in this position today.

Alongside his corporate career, Daniel founded a consulting practice focused on helping business owners scale their companies strategically and sustainably. He has also built and successfully exited his own brick-and-mortar business, giving him firsthand experience as both an operator and an advisor.
